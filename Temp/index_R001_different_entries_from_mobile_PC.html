How It Works

Reference · print once, keep at the front

Paper drives the day. This Week holds only what you've switched on — apps and study both. If it isn't switched on, it isn't this week's problem.

Everything else waits in the Backlog (screen only). Flip a toggle and it rises into This Week; flip it off and it drops back. The Command_centre stays the archive — full history and logs live there, not on paper.

The Weekly Sync

  1. Switch on the apps and topics you'll actually touch; set each active app's priority.
  2. Print the focus sheet (only This Week prints) and work from paper.
  3. End of week: transcribe outcomes into each project's log.md, then re-curate for next week.
